# Gild list rules

1. A completion must be posted on **YouTube** and **have real clicks** (unlisted videos are allowed)
2. The completion must be done on the actual copy of the level posted on my account: **no buffs or nerfs are allowed**
3. Noclip Accuracy 100% and Practice Mode in 1 attempt are **allowed**
4. Harder completions (starting from Shitty Tunnel of Despair) must have a *raw footage* - an unedited recording uploaded to **Google Drive**. You have to provide it when submitting the request by sending me the link and **allowing people who have the link to open the file!**

## Can a level be added to the GILD list?
- Gild56 has to have at least a part or any other participation in that level
- The level must be published on GILD56 gd account
- Levels under 30 seconds are usually added to the Gild Challenges List and longer ones are added to the regular Gild List
